Process

Venus

2024, Digital, 15 in x 18 in

This poster was designed to show one of my favorite songs from this year. To my surprised once I finalized the poster, I sent it to the artist and she replied absolutely loving it.

I wanted to use a gold and black color scheme that promotes the song with the album cover, the length, and the bridge. The numbers represent the release date and I am proud of the framework that I created from scratch with this.
