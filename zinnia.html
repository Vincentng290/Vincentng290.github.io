Process

Zinnia

2024, Digital, 15 in x 18 in

This poster was designed to illustrate my favorite flower. I stumbled upon this flower as I was completing a large project that required the research of countless flowers. When I read about the zinnia and saw it, it became my favorite instantly.

I wanted to create a dark-toned poster compared to my others brighter posters so the bright zinnia with the black background worked very well. It serves as an infographic with the body text explaining when the flower blooms, what it means, and where they originate
